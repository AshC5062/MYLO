Our Goal.

The team is creating the Tesla of longboards. We want to construct a gesture controlled electric longboard that makes riding the board simpler and safer. Using materials such as carbon fiber, bamboo, and kevlar we intend on making the board super lightweight but also super durable. It will also be waterproof, allowing the user to ride in any weather condition.

Our Mission.

Mylo isn't just trying to make the future of how people commute. Longboarding in populated urban areas often leads to accidents that can potentially be life threatening. We want to ensure that neither the rider nor other drivers and civilians get hurt. Through hands free, gesture controling technology, we want to ensure people can safely get around using longboards.
